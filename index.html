LittleNeuron

Infant Visual Stimulation
Mobile Application

A newborn can see clearly within 13 inches from their face and can follow a specific object from 10 to 13 inches.
The high-contrast colors allow the baby to distinguish the shapes from the background. These colors register powerfully on a baby's retina and send strong signals to the brain. Stronger signals = faster visual development & brain growth.
The movements challenge your baby to stay focused on the shape that they are most interested in.

SightWords

Learn to Read
Mobile Application

SightWords is designed to help your child learn & recognize words on sight. Word packs consist of cards, each with its own word. Simply swipe through to each card to view all the words. If your child comes across a word they do not recognize, just press & hold the card, and a friendly voice will read the word aloud! With our simple word pack generator, any parent or teacher can create a word pack for your child to practice with. Learning to read has never been this fun & easy!
